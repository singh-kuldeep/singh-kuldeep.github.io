Mobile Controlled Switch

Here's a very simple device built by Kuldeep Singh (4th year, aerospace and past manager of Aeromodelling club) and Chirag Shetty(4th year, Electrical and past overall coordinator of SATB). We observed the problem of switching the pumps on and off in my village in Rajasthan, while at home in the vacations. The sarpanch of the village had to employ a person solely to go, switch on the pump, stay there for 2-3 hr and switch it off. After coming back, we designed a simple device in TL using the Arduino kit and GSM module and made switching possible using a mobile miss-call; It worked. Then we bought the necessary things, built one and sent it to my village. It has been functioning wonderfully since January '16. This was also the part of presentation that was given at Rashtrapathi Bhavan on Innovation at IITB in March by our team member Chetan Agarwal (past manager of Robotics Club).
